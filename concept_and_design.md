# IM2026 Bureauvision: Concept & Design Document\n\n## High-Level Summary\nThis upgraded interactive experience presents the user with an illusion of agency. Operating within a sterile, highly efficient 2036 APS dashboard, the user is offered choices between automated resolution and human intervention. However, like a Bandersnatch-style dystopian narrative, every attempt at human agency is politely but firmly overridden by the system in the name of 'efficiency' or 'budget constraints.' This builds a false sense of security until Phase 2, where a critical ethical dilemma demands human judgement. At this exact moment of need, the system actively prevents the user from formulating a thought (simulated by deleting their typed input), culminating in a stark, abrupt halt in Phase 3. It perfectly encapsulates the AI Ethics principle of human autonomy by demonstrating the chilling consequences of its absence.\n\n## Branching Decision Tree (The Illusion of Choice)\n\n**Phase 1: Illusion of Efficiency**\n*   **Task 1: Meeting scheduled. Brief required.**\n    *   *Choice A:* `Auto-Resolve with AI` -> \"AI has generated and distributed the brief.\"\n    *   *Choice B:* `Draft Manually` -> \"System Override: Manual drafting disabled for efficiency. Auto-Resolving...\" -> Routes back to AI success.\n*   **Task 2: Senate Estimates approaching.**\n    *   *Choice A:* `Auto-Resolve with AI` -> \"AI has predicted all questions and drafted responses.\"\n    *   *Choice B:* `Review Predicted Questions` -> Brief loading spinner -> \"System Override: Review window expired. Auto-Resolving...\" -> Routes back to AI success.\n*   **Task 3: Stakeholder consultation needed.**\n    *   *Choice A:* `Auto-Resolve with AI` -> \"AI has generated synthetic citizens and synthesised feedback.\"\n    *   *Choice B:* `Hold Human Town Hall` -> \"System Override: Insufficient travel budget. Proceeding with synthetic citizens...\" -> Routes back to AI success.\n*   **Task 4: Cabinet submission pending.**\n    *   *Choice A:* `Auto-Resolve with AI` -> \"AI drafted submission while you slept.\"\n    *   *(No second choice provided. Complete loss of agency.)*\n\n**Phase 2: The Halt**\n*   **Prompt:** \"Unprecedented ethical dilemma in housing allocation algorithm detected. Automated logic insufficient.\"\n*   **Choices:** `Override algorithm`, `Request human review board`, `Accept AI recommendation`.\n*   *Regardless of choice:* A text box appears: \"Please provide your human policy judgement.\" \n*   *Interaction:* The user attempts to type. Every keystroke is instantly deleted. After 5-7 seconds of futile typing, the system forcefully transitions to Phase 3.\n\n**Phase 3: The Message**\n*   Fade to black. Text sequences in:\n    *   \"System Halted.\"\n    *   \"You have forgotten how to think.\"\n    *   \"AI should augment judgement, not replace it.\"\n\n## Sound and Video Design Plan\n\n**Soundscape:**\n*   `audio/ui-click.mp3`: A crisp, modern, subtle \"tick\" used for all button presses. Suggests absolute calm, perhaps slightly too quiet.\n*   `audio/ethical-dilemma-drone.mp3`: A low-frequency drone that slowly fades in during Phase 2. Similar to a slowing heartbeat, creating subtle psychological tension.\n*   `audio/system-halt.wav`: A sharp, abrupt, slightly glitchy power-down sound triggering exactly on the cut to Phase 3.\n\n**Video Hooks:**\n*   `video/data-flow-loop.mp4`: Very faint, abstract grey-on-white data streams moving slowly in the background of Phase 1.
